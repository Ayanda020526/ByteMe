# Product Vision:Smart Quiz Learning Platform 

## Problem Statement
Students struggle to identify their weak areas and often rely on passive learning methods.
There is a lack of systems that provide personalised and interactive study experiences.

## Target Users
-University students 
-High school learners
-Self-learners

## Unique Value Preporsition
A smart quiz that adapts to user performance and generate quizzes from the study material,
helping students study more effectively.

## Key Features 
- Adaptive quizzes
- Perfrmance tracking
- AI generated questions
- Gamifications(points,badges)
- Cross platform access

## Success Metrics
- Quiz completion rate
- Number of active users
- Improvement in user scores
- User engagement levels

## Moore's Vision Statement
FOR: Students who will need effective study tools. 
WHO: Students who struggle with identifying weak areas and staying engaged
THE:Smart Quiz Learning Platform.
IS A:Cross platform quiz based learning system.
THAT: Helps users improve throuhg adaptive quizzes and AI generated content 
UNLIKE:Traditional study methods that are passive and non-personalised.
OUR PRODUCT: Provides interactive, personalised, and effective learning experiences.
