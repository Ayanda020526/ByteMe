# User Personas and Scenarios

## Persona 1: Ayanda (University Student)

- **Age**: 21  
- **Occupation**: Computer Science Student  
- **Technical Level**: Intermediate (comfortable with apps and technology)  
- **Goals**:
  - Improve understanding of course material
  - Prepare effectively for tests and exams
  - Identify weak areas in her studies  
- **Frustrations**:
  - Studying without knowing if she truly understands content
  - Lack of interactive learning tools
  - Difficulty tracking academic progress over time  

### Scenario
Ayanda has an upcoming test and wants to revise efficiently. Instead of only reading notes, she logs into the Smart Quiz app and selects a quiz related to her module. 
As she answers questions, the system provides an interactive experience that keeps her engaged.  

After completing the quiz, she immediately receives her score along with feedback highlighting incorrect answers. 
The system also shows her performance trends, helping her identify weak topics. 
Ayanda uses this information to focus her revision on areas where she needs improvement, making her study sessions more effective and targeted.

## Persona 2: Mr. Dlamini (Lecturer)

- **Age**: 40  
- **Occupation**: University Lecturer  
- **Technical Level**: Moderate (uses digital tools for teaching)  
- **Goals**:
  - Provide students with effective practice materials
  - Monitor student performance
  - Identify common areas where students struggle  
- **Frustrations**:
  - Difficulty tracking individual student progress
  - Limited tools for interactive assessments
  - Time-consuming manual grading processes  

### Scenario
Mr. Dlamini wants to help his students better prepare for exams. He logs into the Smart Quiz platform and creates a quiz based on recent lecture topics.
He uploads multiple-choice questions and assigns the quiz to his class.  

After students complete the quiz, Mr. Dlamini reviews the analytics provided by the system.
He notices that many students performed poorly on a specific topic. Using this insight, he revisits that topic in class and provides additional explanations. 
This allows him to improve teaching effectiveness and better support his students.

## Persona 3: Sipho (High School Learner)

- **Age**: 17  
- **Occupation**: High School Student  
- **Technical Level**: Basic to Intermediate (frequent mobile app user)  
- **Goals**:
  - Improve school performance
  - Practice subjects in a fun and engaging way
  - Stay motivated while studying  
- **Frustrations**:
  - Traditional studying feels boring
  - Lack of instant feedback when practicing
  - Difficulty staying consistent with studying  

### Scenario
After school, Sipho uses his phone to access the Smart Quiz app. He selects a subject he wants to practice and starts a quiz. 
The app presents questions in a simple and engaging format, making learning feel more like a game.  

As he completes quizzes, he earns scores and tracks his progress. The instant feedback helps him understand mistakes immediately
which keeps him motivated. Over time, Sipho notices improvement in his performance at school, making the app a regular part of his study routine.
